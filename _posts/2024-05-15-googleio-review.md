#### Summary

The battle lines are now drawn: 'OpenAI + MS + Apple' vs 'Google'.

The announcements at Google I/O 2024 today — including the Gemini app (replacing Google Assistant with Gemini), Search (+Android), Project Astra, Google Workspace, and media models (Veo) — represent Google's comprehensive response to what OpenAI, MS, Apple and others have each announced separately.

Since modern people use Google Search, Gmail, Chrome, and Android phones every day, they cannot escape Google services, which makes today's Google announcements more impactful on digital life and noticeable in terms of change.

The most revolutionary change is still 'Search'. The words of Liz Reid, Google's head of Search — 'Google will do the Googling for you' — were impressive. Through 'AI Overviews', Google Search offers quick summaries and related links for search results. The goal is to help find answers to complex questions... and it is expected to change the way people access and interpret information.

How people will use Google Search going forward and how it will change remains to be seen, but as the way information is accessed changes, the digital ecosystem that has formed over the past 20 years will inevitably change as well. The search market is gradually declining. Many businesses that depend on search advertising may find themselves in trouble.
