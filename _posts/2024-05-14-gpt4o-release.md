## GPT-4o: OpenAI's Revolutionary Multimodal LLM

*Curiosity:* What happens when a single model can seamlessly understand and generate across text, audio, and vision in real-time? How does this innovation reshape human-AI interaction?

## 🤯 OpenAI's New Multimodal LLM Can Understand and Generate Text, Audio, and Vision in Real-Time.

### 💬🗣️👀 Here Is What We Know So Far.


#### Details
- OpenAI has announced a new GPT-4o model that outperforms GPT-4, but most importantly, it's available for free to all users! 🚀
⠀
- An updated interface and PC application with voice control and screen sharing capabilities have been introduced 🎤
⠀
- Natively multimodal: text, image, and voice generation are now handled by a single model 🗣️.
⠀
- Developers are not forgotten either, as it supports an API that is 2x faster, 50% cheaper, and has 5x higher rate limits compared to GPT-4 💰
⠀
- Voice mode has been significantly improved, allowing interruption of model generation at any time without waiting for it to finish. OpenAI has also elevated voice generation to real-time levels, and most importantly, ChatGPT Voice has been made truly vivid! 🎶
⠀
- There is also an interactive mode that allows you to simultaneously share camera images and communicate with ChatGPT! 💬
⠀

- 📥 Input: Text, Text + Image, Text + Audio, Text + Video, Audio (example-based)
- 📤 Output: Image, Image + Text, Text, Audio (example-based)

- 🖼️ 69.1% on MMU; 92.8% on DocVQA

- 🎤 Near real-time audio with 320ms on average, similar to human conversation
